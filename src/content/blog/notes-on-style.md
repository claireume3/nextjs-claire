Style, to me, is less about following a trend and more about consistency — the same eye for tailoring, the same restraint in color, the same instinct for when _less_ actually is more.

A short list of what I tend to look for:

- Clean lines over anything overly embellished
- Texture that photographs well in low light
- One statement piece, never two

Everything else is negotiable.

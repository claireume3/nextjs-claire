Chicago has a way of feeling both grand and intimate at once — glass towers leaning over a river that moves slower than the city around it.

I keep a small ritual whenever I'm in town: breakfast at the same table, a walk along the same bridge, and an evening that's never quite planned. **This trip was no exception.**

Some of my favorite frames from the last few years have come from exactly this kind of unplanned wandering. A flower arrangement caught mid-afternoon light. A barge easing under a drawbridge. None of it staged — all of it kept.

If you've followed my [travel gallery](/gallery/selfies-travel), you'll recognize a few corners of this city already.
